Welcome to

Hide Out Farm

We invite you to surrender to the power of nature in our sacred fruit forest. Rejuvenate your senses in our sustainably built home that is open to the positive energy of the earth. We encourage guests to book long stays with us to truly detox, unwind, and refresh themselves.

Hideout Farm is not just a mere farmhouse, B&B, or a weekend getaway close to Mumbai. We are a wellness destination dedicated to the healing and well being of everyone who arrives here. This farm-stay is our family home where we live and share our space with an open mind and heart.
